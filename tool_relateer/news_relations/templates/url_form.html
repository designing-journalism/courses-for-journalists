Add and Relate News Article URL
This tool allows you to add new articles to your collection and relate them to all existing articles.

Enter the URL of a news article to add it to your collection, generate a summary, and find related articles in the database.

Enter the full URL including https://
How It Works
  1. Enter the URL of a news article
  2. The system will fetch and parse the article content
  3. The article will be added to your collection if it's not already present
  4. An AI-generated summary will be created to give you a quick overview
  5. Using vector embeddings, it will compare the article against all existing articles in your collection
  6. AI will analyze the relationships between the article and the most similar existing articles
  7. Results will be displayed with visualizations to help understand the connections
Tip: Make sure you've indexed your articles first by clicking the "Index All Articles" button on the home page.
