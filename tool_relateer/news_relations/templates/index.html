About News Relations

This tool helps you discover relationships between news articles. You can:

  • Browse existing news articles and see related content
  • Analyze a new URL to find related articles in the database
  • Visualize relationships between articles

The system uses AI to analyze the semantic relationships between articles and provides visualizations to help understand these connections.

This will create embeddings for all articles to enable similarity search.
